How It Works

The Web Dev Rebellion lives on your computer, in your own filesystem.

But we're not stuck in the past. You'll learn to use Cursor, the modern code editor (think VS Code, but built for the AI era). Cursor gives you the power of AI assistance right in your editor, helping you write code faster while you stay in control. It's the perfect bridge between old-school fundamentals and modern efficiency.

Here's how it works: You download the learning system to your computer. It's a collection of files—lessons, examples, projects—that you can open, read, modify, and learn from at your own pace. You work through the material using Cursor, building real websites as you go. Everything lives on your machine. You own it completely.
