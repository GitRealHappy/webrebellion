This lesson will introduce the philosophy of Web Design Rebellion. We are moving away from sterile, corporate frameworks and reclaiming the raw creative power of the web.

## Key Concepts

1. **Own Your Stack**: Don't rely on black boxes.
2. **Code is Art**: HTML and CSS are your medium.
3. **Build for Humans**: Simplicity and speed over complexity.
