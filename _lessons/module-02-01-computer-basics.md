### Get Your Digital Toolkit Ready

Coding happens on a computer. Knowing how to wield your machine is foundational. This guide cuts through the noise to get you the essential computer knowledge you need to start building.
</div>

<div class="lesson-block" markdown="1">
### What You'll Master

- **Core Components:** Understand the difference between hardware and software – the building blocks of your digital world.
- **Visual Communication:** Learn to capture screenshots – a crucial skill for documenting progress and sharing issues.
- **Digital Security:** Grasp the non-negotiable importance of strong passwords.
</div>

<div class="lesson-block" markdown="1">

  Grasping the fundamental hardware and software concepts.

  Knowing what an OS does and recognizing key examples.
- **Understanding Applications**
  Differentiating between system software and user-facing applications.
- **Open vs. Closed Source Software**
  A quick look at software philosophies.
- **Taking Screenshots**
  Mastering this essential visual tool.
- **Creating Strong Passwords**
  Your first line of defense in the digital realm.
